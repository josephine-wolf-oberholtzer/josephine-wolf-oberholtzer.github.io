Joséphine Wolf Oberholtzer 🏳️‍⚧️


is a composer, researcher, and software developer


She resides in Kingston, New York with her husband and Bradley, their German shepherd dog.

She's currently employed as a staff engineer for Cortico, where she contributes to the backend, infrastructure, and overall software architecture.


Research interests include:

  • Distributed systems architecture
  • Machine listening
  • Digital musicology
  • Symbolic music composition
  • Electro-acoustic composition and performance
  • Multi-media and live-electronics toolkit development
